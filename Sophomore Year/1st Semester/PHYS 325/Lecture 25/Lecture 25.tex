We define generalized coordinates 
\begin{equation}
    \{q_i\} = \{q_1, \dots\}
\end{equation}
A generalized velocity:
\begin{equation}
    \dot{q_i} = \frac{d}{dt}q_i
\end{equation}
Note, $\{q_i\}$ is a list of potential paths. We introduce an action as a functional 
\begin{equation}
    S[\vec{q}(t)] = \int_{t_1}^{t_2}dtL(q_i(t), \dot{q}_i(t), t)
\end{equation}
